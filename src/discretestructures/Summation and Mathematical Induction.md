**Sigma Notation**

* To express the sum of the terms of the sequence a~n~ = {a~m~,a~m+1~,...,a~n~}, we write:

  $\displaystyle\sum_{i=m}^{n} a_{i}$ = $\sum_{i=m}^{n} a_{i}$ = $\displaystyle\sum_{m \leq i \leq n} a_{i}$

  which represents a~m~ + ,a~m+1~ + ,a~m+2~ + a~n~
